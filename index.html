🚀 Implementation Roadmap

4-Phase Implementation Strategy

1 Foundation (Weeks 1-4)

Priority: Revenue-generating flows with immediate impact

Flows: Welcome Series (8 emails), Cart Abandonment (5 emails), Checkout Abandonment (duplicate), Basic Post-Purchase (3 emails)

Success Metric: 15%+ increase in automated revenue within 30 days

2 Intelligence (Weeks 5-8)

Priority: AI-powered personalization and advanced segmentation

Features: Dynamic content, predictive send times, advanced segmentation, conditional logic

Success Metric: 25%+ improvement in flow conversion rates

3 Retention (Weeks 9-12)

Priority: Customer lifecycle optimization and loyalty building

Flows: Advanced Post-Purchase (8 emails), Winback (5 emails), VIP Flow, Replenishment, Review flows

Success Metric: 20%+ increase in customer lifetime value

4 Innovation (Weeks 13-16)

Priority: Advanced 2025 features and omnichannel integration

Features: Quiz flows, Back-in-stock alerts, SMS coordination, Interactive elements, Advanced analytics

Success Metric: 40%+ total automated revenue increase from baseline
